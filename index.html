The Triangle tracker

What is a triangle?

In a simple explanation, a triangle is any plane figure with three straight sides and three angles.

Therefore, a triangle tracker is an application which is meant to detect the type of triangle when user puts in the values of the three sides of the triangle.

Types of triangles:

We have three types of triangles:

  1. Equilateral triangleThis is a triangle that has three equal sides and equal angles.
  2. Isosceles triangle This is a triangle that has two equal sides.
  3. Scalene triangle This is a triangle that has three different angles and none of its three sides have equal length.
